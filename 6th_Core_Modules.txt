Core Modules :- 1) Global Modules
                2) Local Modules

1) Global Modules :-    Do not Require to import them.
                        They are Globally present everywhere.
                        E.X. :- console. alert, etc.

2) Local Modules :-     Required to import them.
                        They are not Globally present everywhere.
                        E.X. :- fs, HTTP, etc.
                                const fs = require("fs)
